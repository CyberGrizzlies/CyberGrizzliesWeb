About our Competitive teams

We will have one team per sport and then will add more if there is interest.

Teams will compete against other Colleges and Universities across North America.

Games we usually have teams for are: League Of Legends, CS: GO, Rocket League, Valorant, Overwatch, and Rainbow Six Siege.
